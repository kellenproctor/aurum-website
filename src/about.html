Aurum - what we're about

We’re a small marketing company. While we have experience working with large brands, our happy place is working with entrepreneurs and other small businesses to help make their dreams a reality. Working backwards from your business goals, we execute on everything from targeted, scrappy social strategies to aggressive, multi-platform approaches to scale. We’re your partners in this, so you’ll hear from us often and be fully in the loop on exactly what’s happening in your account.
